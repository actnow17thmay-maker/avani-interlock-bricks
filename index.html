Engineered to make building easier

Traditional bricks need sand, cement plastering, painting and days of water curing. Avani interlock bricks remove all of that — saving time, money and effort on every project.

Skip days of watering. Bricks are ready to use, keeping your site clean and on schedule.

No Cement Plastering

No Sand Needed

Faster Construction

Bricks interlock and stack quickly, so walls rise faster with less labour.

Easy To Lay

How It Works

Four simple steps to a stronger wall

  1. 01

    Lay & Interlock

    Bricks slot together with tongue-and-groove edges, keeping every course perfectly aligned.

  2. 02

    Minimal Mortar

    Only a thin bonding layer is needed — no sand-heavy mix, far less waste.

  3. 03

    No Plastering

    The smooth faces mean the wall is finished as it's built — no rendering stage.

  4. 04

    Ready To Paint

    Apply paint directly onto a clean, even surface. Done faster, looks better.
